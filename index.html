About CodTech

CodTech is an innovative online learning platform designed to equip interns and aspiring developers with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in today's technology landscape. Our platform offers high-quality content, including video tutorials, quizzes, certificates, and progress tracking to ensure a rich and interactive learning experience.
